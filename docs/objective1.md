# Objective 1: Patient and Appointment Management Module

**Owner:** Lalo, John Patrick Russel D.  
**Module Title:** Patient and Appointment Management  

## Objective Description
The objective of the Patient and Appointment Management module is to provide an efficient and centralized system for managing patient records and clinic appointments. It enables the digital recording, retrieval, and updating of patient information while documenting consultation details and eye checkup findings. The module also streamlines appointment scheduling, monitors doctor availability, sends notifications to relevant stakeholders, and uses AI-assisted summarization to support faster and more informed clinical decision-making.
